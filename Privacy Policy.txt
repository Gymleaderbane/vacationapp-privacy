Privacy Policy
Effective Date: 4/2/2025
1. Introduction
Welcome to VacationApp ("the App"). Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use the App.
2. Information We Collect
When you use our App, we may collect the following types of information:
•	Account Information: When you register, we collect your username and password (stored securely using encryption methods).
•	Vacation Data: Users may enter details about vacations and excursions, which are stored locally on the device.
•	Automatically Collected Data: We may collect anonymous app usage statistics to improve functionality.
3. How We Use Your Information
We use the collected data for the following purposes:
•	To authenticate and manage user accounts.
•	To store and retrieve vacation details for user convenience.
•	To improve the App based on user interactions.
4. Data Storage and Security
•	All user data, including vacation details, is stored locally on the device in a SQLite database.
•	Passwords are stored securely using hashing techniques.
•	We do not share or sell user data to third parties.
•	If the App connects to a cloud service in the future, users will be notified about any changes in data storage policies.
5. Account Deletion
Users may delete their account and associated data by using the "Delete Account" feature on the login screen. Once deleted, this data cannot be recovered.
6. Third-Party Services
Currently, the App does not integrate with third-party services that collect user data. If any such integrations occur in the future, this policy will be updated accordingly.
7. User Rights
•	You have the right to delete your account and associated data at any time.
•	You can contact us for any questions regarding your data and privacy rights.
8. Changes to This Policy
We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Users will be notified of significant changes within the App.
9. Contact Us
If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at hammerholdtales@gmail.com.
